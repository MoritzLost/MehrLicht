### General

This website does not use cookies, analytics tools, or tracking technologies. No personal data is actively collected or stored by the website operator.

When you visit this website, technical data is automatically processed for the purpose of delivering the website content. This includes:

## Your rights

Under the GDPR, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

- **Right of access** (Article 15 GDPR): You can request information about the personal data processed about you.
- **Right to rectification** (Article 16 GDPR): You can request correction of inaccurate personal data.
- **Right to erasure** (Article 17 GDPR): You can request deletion of your personal data under certain circumstances.
- **Right to restriction of processing** (Article 18 GDPR): You can request restriction of processing under certain circumstances.
- **Right to data portability** (Article 20 GDPR): You can request to receive your data in a structured, commonly used format.
- **Right to object** (Article 21 GDPR): You can object to processing based on legitimate interests.

To exercise these rights, please contact [m@moritzlost.dev](mailto:m@moritzlost.dev).

## Right to lodge a complaint

You have the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority if you believe that the processing of your personal data violates the GDPR. In Germany, you can contact your state's data protection authority.

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time to reflect changes in data processing practices or legal requirements. The current version is always available on this page.
